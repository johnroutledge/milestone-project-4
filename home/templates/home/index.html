About JIIRA Jewellery

Conceived in an art studio five years ago, Jiira Jewellery is the birthchild of fine art student Jiratchaya Chaiyasak. Being a lover of anything that combines art with beauty, Jiratchaya discovered the perfect manifestation of this when she started designing jewellery. Having been taught how to fashion jewellery from silver by one of the most renowned art teachers in northern Thailand, she was able to give life to her designs.


Every piece is lovingly created by hand with materials ethically sourced exclusively from Thailand. Since each piece is painstakingly made by hand, you can be sure that your purchase is always intrinsically unique. Jiira Jewellery provides stunning pieces that combine timeless looks with a touch of Siamese beauty.
